IEEE SPS KERALA

The IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS) Kerala Chapter is a vibrant professional community dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in the field of signal processing. The chapter actively engages students, researchers, academicians, and industry professionals through a wide range of technical activities, including workshops, webinars, hands-on sessions, conferences, and outreach programs. It aims to bridge the gap between theory and real-world applications by promoting emerging areas such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, image and speech processing, biomedical signal processing, and communication systems. With a strong focus on student development, the IEEE SPS Kerala Chapter encourages leadership, research culture, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Through hub-level conclaves, competitions, and mentorship initiatives, the chapter nurtures technical excellence and professional growth while contributing to the global vision and standards of the IEEE Signal Processing Society.

EVENT HOST: TKMCE

T. K. M. College of Engineering (TKMCE), Kollam is a prestigious government-aided engineering institution established in 1958 under the TKM College Trust, with its foundation stone laid by Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the first President of India. It is located in Karicode, Kollam, Kerala, and is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE. The college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, and B.Arch, and also supports doctoral research in several disciplines. TKMCE has strong academic credentials with NBA accreditation for its engineering programs, NAAC “A” grade, and consistent rankings among top engineering institutions in India. The campus provides well-equipped labs, libraries, hostels, sports and extracurricular facilities, and a dedicated placement cell that connects students with top recruiters. With a legacy of excellence in technical education and a vibrant student community, TKMCE nurtures professional and personal growth.
